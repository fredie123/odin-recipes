Banana Bread

Origin of Banana bread

Banana bread is a type of sweet bread or cake made from mashed bananas.It is often a moist and sweet quick bread but some recipes are yeast raised, wiki. To be honest I don't know the origin yet

Banana bread comes in variation, cool right? I'm gonna share how i make mine.

Ingredients

  • Bananas
  • Egg
  • Sugar
  • Melted Butter
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Milk, optional
  • Flour
  • Baking soda
  • Cinnamon
  • Salt

Steps

All ingredients should be properly measured and at room temp. Preheat the oven at 350F for 10 to 15mins

  1. Mix the dry ingredients, flour, bakind soda, cinnamon and salt and set aside.
  2. Mash the bananas in a bowl, add the melted butter, sugar, egg, vanilla extract and milk
  3. Mix the wet ingredients.
  4. Gently pour half of the dry into the wet and mix
  5. Add the second half and mix till its fully incorporated i.eyou can't see any visible flour.
  6. Pour the mixture in the your pan and pop in the oven. Bake for 55mins to 1 hour. Do the toothpick check if its dry then its bye bye oven

Let the bread rest in the pan for like 10-15mins before transferred to a rack for cooling. And there you have it, sweet banana bread served with either milk, juice, beverages or even eaten like that. Yummy right now i am going to make one right away
