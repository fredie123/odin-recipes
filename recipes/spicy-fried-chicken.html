Spicy Fried Chicken

About Spicy Fried Chicken

Chicken is my favorite type of meat and also food. Anything with it sign my mouth up, in stew, shawarma, pie. I love me some chickens and this on of my solo chicken recipes. It made with love and lot of spice.

P.S This is an original, so enjoy

Ingredients

  • Chicken, you can use any part, but i would recommend drumsticks for solos
  • Pepper (habanero pepper and bell pepper)
  • Onions
  • Salt and salt
  • Oil
  • Seasoning, spice of your choice

Steps

Prep your ingredients, wash the washable and cut the cuttable

  1. Marinate the chicken, add spices, salt and pop in the fridge
  2. Add little oil to the pan, pour in onions, fry a little, add pepper,salt. heat a little. Set aside
  3. Clean the pan and add a lot of oil, put the marinated chicken. let it fry for a while. Remove once light golden brown
  4. Get a sauce pan, add the fried chicken and pepper sauce. Fried for a while and turn the off the heat

Spicy chicken with water coming up, if you can take the heat with other liquid be my guest. This is an excellent dish to just eat at home on Friday night binge-watching k-drama
